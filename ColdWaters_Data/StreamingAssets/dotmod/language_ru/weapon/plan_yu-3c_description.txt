WeaponName=Yu-3C
WeaponDescriptiveName=Yu-3C ASW Torpedo
WeaponDescription=The Yu-3C is a domestic Chinese ASW torpedo with a 13km range and a top speed of 35 knots. The combination active/passive homing system has a maximum range of approximately 1,200 yards, and while the weapon's original design specification only called for a maximum depth of 200m, the final product was capable of operating at around 400m.
