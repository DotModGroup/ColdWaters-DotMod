WeaponDescription=Special: Wake-homing\nAnti-ship torpedo. The 53-65K is the upgraded version of the 53-65 torpedo. It has an extended range due to new propulsion system. Wake-homing torpedoes should be aimed to cross wake behind ship's stern. Surfaced submarines can also be hit with WH torpedoes.
